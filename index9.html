Green Tikka



Green Tikka is a popular and flavorful dish known for its vibrant color and aromatic taste. The chicken is marinated in a mixture of fresh cilantro, mint, green chilies, yogurt, and a blend of spices, which gives it a zesty and herby flavor. The green marinade not only adds a beautiful green hue to the chicken but also infuses it with a refreshing, mildly spicy taste. The chicken is then grilled or baked until perfectly cooked, resulting in a tender, juicy, and flavorful bite. Green Tikka is often served with naan or rice, making it a perfect dish for any BBQ or special gathering.
