Chicken Malai Boti



Chicken Malai Boti is a delicious and flavorful Pakistani dish, often prepared for barbecues or grilling. The chicken is marinated in a mixture of cream, yogurt, milk, lemon juice, and a blend of aromatic spices, which makes it tender and succulent. The result is a rich, creamy texture with a mild, yet savory taste that melts in your mouth. It is typically served with warm naan or rice and is a popular choice for special occasions or BBQ gatherings, offering a perfect combination of indulgence and spice.
