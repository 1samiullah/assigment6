Brost



A roast is a flavorful and hearty dish where meat, often beef, chicken, or pork, is cooked slowly in an oven until it becomes tender and juicy on the inside, while developing a golden, crispy exterior. The meat is typically seasoned with herbs, spices, and marinades to enhance its natural flavors. Accompaniments like roasted vegetables, potatoes, and gravy often complete the dish, making it a satisfying and comforting meal. Roasting brings out the best in the ingredients, allowing them to caramelize and develop a deep, rich taste. Whether enjoyed during family gatherings or festive occasions, a roast is a classic meal that never fails to impress
