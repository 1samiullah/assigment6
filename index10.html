Corn Ice Cream



Corn ice cream is a unique and delicious dessert that combines the natural sweetness of corn with the creamy richness of ice cream. Made by blending fresh corn kernels into a smooth custard base, this ice cream offers a subtle yet delightful flavor that is both sweet and slightly savory. The corn adds a surprising texture and depth to the dessert, making it a refreshing and innovative treat. Often garnished with a hint of roasted corn or sweet syrup, corn ice cream is perfect for those looking to try something different and indulge in a sweet, comforting flavor with a twist.
