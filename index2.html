Beef Biryani



Beef Biryani is a rich and flavorful dish made by combining tender pieces of beef with fragrant basmati rice, cooked together with an array of aromatic spices. The beef is marinated with yogurt, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala, allowing it to absorb the deep, savory flavors. The rice is layered with the beef and cooked slowly, allowing the spices to infuse every grain. Often garnished with crispy fried onions, fresh cilantro, and sometimes boiled eggs, beef biryani is a satisfying and indulgent meal that is beloved for its rich taste and comforting nature.
