Chicken Biryani



Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that combines tender chicken with fragrant basmati rice, cooked together with a blend of spices such as saffron, cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. The chicken is marinated with yogurt, garlic, ginger, and a mix of spices, which infuses it with rich flavors. The rice is carefully layered with the chicken, then cooked on low heat to allow the spices to meld and the flavors to develop. Often garnished with fried onions, cilantro, and boiled eggs, chicken biryani is a comforting and delicious meal that is loved by many.
