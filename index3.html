Mutton Biryani



Mutton Biryani is a flavorful and hearty dish made with tender pieces of mutton (goat meat) and aromatic basmati rice, cooked together with a blend of rich spices. The mutton is marinated in a mixture of yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric, allowing it to absorb all the delicious flavors. The rice is layered with the mutton, and the dish is slow-cooked to perfection, letting the spices infuse both the meat and rice. Garnished with crispy fried onions, fresh cilantro, and sometimes boiled eggs, mutton biryani is a comforting and indulgent meal that is loved for its depth of flavor and savory richness.
