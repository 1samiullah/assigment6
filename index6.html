Freanch Fries



French fries are a popular and beloved snack made from potatoes that are cut into thin strips and deep-fried until golden and crispy. They are often seasoned with salt, and sometimes other spices or toppings like cheese, garlic, or ketchup are added for extra flavor. French fries are served as a side dish in many meals, particularly with burgers, sandwiches, or fried chicken, but they can also be enjoyed on their own as a quick snack. Their crispy texture on the outside and soft, fluffy interior make them irresistibly delicious, and they are a favorite comfort food worldwide.
